PuckSlide – Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

PuckSlide (Billar Holandés / Sjoelen) is a Dutch shuffleboard game. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Local (offline) play

No account required: local play does not require registration and does not collect personal information. Player names entered before a local match are used only to label local scores and are never sent anywhere.

PuckSlide stores the following data locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage):

  • Best scores per player name (the device's local high-score record)
  • Sound on/off preference

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Online play (optional)

PuckSlide also offers an optional online mode with leaderboards. To use it, you must create an account by signing in with Google or with an email and password. If you choose to use online play, we collect and store the following data using Supabase (our database and authentication provider):

  • Your email address (used for login)
  • The display name and country you choose when creating your profile
  • Your match scores, used to calculate leaderboards

This data is used only to operate the online leaderboards (showing your name, country and score to other players) and is not sold or shared with third parties, other than Supabase, which processes it on our behalf as our database provider.

Account and data deletion (PuckSlide)

To delete your PuckSlide online account and all associated data (profile, display name, country, match history and login credentials), follow these steps:

  1. Open PuckSlide, go to the online hub (sign in first if needed), and tap "Request account deletion", or from the main menu tap "Contact" and send us a message asking for your account to be deleted.
  2. Alternatively, send an email to dhaudiovisuales@gmail.com with the subject line "PuckSlide account deletion", from the email address linked to your PuckSlide account.

What is deleted: your login credentials (email/Google account link), profile (display name and country), and your full match/score history are all permanently deleted, with no backups kept beyond the deletion window below.

What is retained, and for how long: nothing is retained after deletion. We will process and complete your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it; after that point none of your data remains in our systems.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob – the Android version of PuckSlide displays ads via Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers (such as the advertising ID) and usage data for advertising purposes. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Supabase – used to authenticate accounts (Google sign-in or email/password) and store online profiles and scores. See Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Children's privacy

PuckSlide is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally, for example when new features are added. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
